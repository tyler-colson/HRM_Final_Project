Rules to Build Your Credit


  • 1. Get a secured card
  • 2. Get a credit-builder product or a secured loan

  • 3. Use a co-signer

  • 4. Get credit for the bills you pay

  • 5. Practice good credit habits
  • 6. Check your credit scores and reports
  • 7. Become an authorized user


General Rules for Credit Cards


  • 1. Pay your credit card bill on time

  • 2. Pay your credit card in full

  • 3. Keep credit card utilization rate low
  • 4. Only charge what you can afford
  • 5. Read your statements each month
  • 6. Choose cards that suit your needs
  • 7. Avoid cards with annual fees

  • 8. Don't close credit cards
